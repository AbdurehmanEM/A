Selected Publications

Multiorgan-on-a-chip for cancer drug pharmacokinetics-pharmacodynamics (PK-PD) modeling and simulations

This study developed a 3D-printed integrated breast-liver multiorgan-on-a-chip platform to experimentally generate pharmacokinetic and pharmacodynamic data for cancer drugs. The data was then modeled computationally to identify optimal and safe dosing regimens. This single-fabrication system offers a robust and human-relevant alternative to animal models for the preclinical evaluation of drug efficacy and toxicity.

M. tuberculosis in Lymph Node Biopsy Paraffin-Embedded Sections

Our study at Jimma University Specialized Hospital confirmed a high prevalence (61.7%) of tuberculosis lymphadenitis in lymph node biopsies, with a significantly higher occurrence in female patients, necessitating enhanced control and prevention efforts.

Diabetes mellitus and risk factors in human immunodeficiency virus-infected individuals at Jimma University Specialized Hospital, Southwest Ethiopia

This cross-sectional study conducted at Jimma University Specialized Hospital found that 6.4% of adults infected with 393 HIV have diabetes mellitus.

The key risk factors significantly associated with diabetes include older age, a longer duration of HAART (highly active antiretroviral therapy), hypertension, and high LDL cholesterol levels.

The study concludes that individuals infected with HIV should be screened for diabetes both before and after starting HAART, as the treatment may affect their risk for developing diabetes.
